About Garaad.org
At Garaad.org, we believe that education is not just about memorizing facts; it is about building the mental "machine" needed to navigate a complex world. Our name, Garaad, reflects the Somali value of "wisdom" and "intellect"—a quality that, like the best systems, is earned through merit and constant evolution.
Inspired by the principles that drive world-class performance, we’ve built Garaad.org on three foundational pillars:
Radical Truth in Learning: We don't shield learners from the "hard" parts of STEM. We embrace the reality that progress requires struggle. By making logic and problem-solving transparent, we empower students to see the world as it truly is.
Pain + Reflection = Progress: We treat every wrong answer as a data point for growth. Our platform is designed to help students identify their own "blind spots," diagnose the root causes of their mistakes, and iterate until they reach mastery.
The Idea Meritocracy of Knowledge: We believe the best solutions should rise to the top. Garaad.org provides a platform where Somali students, regardless of background, can compete and collaborate based on the quality of their thinking and their dedication to the truth.
Our Mission
To systemize the way the Somali generation learns, ensuring that every student has the tools to think for themselves and decide what is true and what they should do to achieve their goals.
